Introduction

Welcome to the introduction of my interaction design project, focused on gamifying a learning app. our main aim is to make the learning experience more engaging and interactive for our users.

Gamification involves applying game design principles to non-game contexts, such as education. The objective is to increase user motivation, engagement, and enjoyment.

This project involved various phases, including a competitive analysis, wireframing, prototyping, and usability testing. The competitive analysis helped me identify strengths and weaknesses in the existing market, and highlighted areas we can improve

Wireframing and prototyping allowed us to design and develop the interface and user experience of the app, ensuring a mooth and enjoyable learning experience.

Finally, usability testing was conducted to gather feedback from users on the app's usability and functionality. The feedback will be used to refine and improve the app, ensuring that the user's needs are met

Overall, our aim is to create a learning app that is not only effective but also enjoyable, iltimately improving the user's learning outcomes.
